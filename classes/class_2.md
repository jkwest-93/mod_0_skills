# Class: Chair

## Attributes
material (string) </br>
numberOfLegs (integer) </br>
isOpen (boolean) </br>
height (float) </br>

## Methods
remodel (changes the material attribute) </br>
breakLeg (subtracts 1 from numberOfLegs) </br>
seatCustomer (changes isOpen from true to false) </br>
addBoosterSeat (adds 8 to height)
